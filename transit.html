Transit Links

  TTC

The Toronto Transit Commission, or TTC, is responsible for providing public transit in the City of Toronto. Toronto is 622 square kilometres in size, located in the centre of the Greater Toronto Area, and has a population of 2.8 million people. The TTC serves this area with a grid network of four subway lines, 11 streetcar routes, and more than 140 bus routes.

 Go Transit

GO Transit is a network of train and bus lines that keep people moving around the Greater Toronto and Hamilton Area.

 Google Maps

Google Maps is a Web-based service that provides detailed information about geographical regions and sites around the world. In addition to conventional road maps, Google Maps offers aerial and satellite views of many places. In some cities, Google Maps offers street views comprising photographs taken from vehicles.
